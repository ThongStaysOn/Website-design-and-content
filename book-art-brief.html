What Generates Transactions on Ink

Character skins and cosmetics - the highest-volume, lowest-friction entry point. Players buy, trade, and flex thousands of combinations.

Furniture and rooms - every decoration purchase is an onchain transaction. Seasonal drops create recurring buying cycles.

Minigame rewards - every game earns tickets. Tickets earn rewards. Rewards have real value on Ink.

Pokek poker - onchain settlement. Real stakes, real pots, high-value transactions.

How a Retail User Ends Up on Ink
New
7-step funnel
Discovers the game - through Epic Games Store, social media, or a friend
Plays as a guest - no wallet needed. Explores the city, plays games, customizes a character
Wants to keep what they earned - items, skins, tickets. The game prompts: "connect a wallet to save your progress"
Connects a wallet - through Kraken Wallet or any supported option. First onchain moment.
Bridges to Ink - guided flow inside the game. Feels like part of the experience, not a separate step.
Starts owning and trading - mints a skin, buys furniture, trades an item P2P. Every action is a transaction on Ink.
Keeps coming back - daily quests, seasonal events, community gatherings. One-time user becomes a daily active wallet.
