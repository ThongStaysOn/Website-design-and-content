## Slide: About the Game (02-vision/about-what-is-kekspace.png)

**Section:** Vision (Section 2)

**Old copy:**
> Sub-heading: "A world that feels like home"
> Body: "Kekspace is a social virtual world powered by Pepecoin where players hang out, play games, collect items, and own everything on-chain. Think of the social worlds you grew up in — rebuilt with real digital ownership."
> "It's built for communities, creators, degens, and curious newcomers who want a space that's as fun as it is functional. Every item you earn, every room you build, every skin you wear — it's yours."

**New copy (APPROVED):**
> Sub-heading: "The social world crypto was supposed to build."
> Body: "A whole world waiting for you. Shops to browse, games to play, rooms to decorate, people to meet — and everything you collect is yours to keep. Powered by Pepecoin, filled by the community."

**Notes:**
- Replaced two paragraphs with one — tighter, more inviting
- Shifted from comparative/contrast tone to excitement/invitation
- Paints the picture of the world instead of explaining what it is
- "Filled by the community" echoes the welcome slide's "built by the people in it"

## Slide: About the Game — Full Q&A rewrite (02-vision/about-what-is-kekspace.png)

**Section:** Vision (Section 2)

### Block 1: "What is Kekspace?"

**Original (KEEPING AS-IS):** "Kekspace is a social interface for Web3 that blends the social elements of early 2000's social MMOs with Web3/DeFi utility."

### Block 2: "What can you do in Kekspace?"

**Old:** "Play games, collect items, create your own virtual rooms, hang out with frens, access DeFi apps, and contribute funds, assets, and more!"

**New (APPROVED):** "Play games, customize your character, decorate your own room, hang out with the community, and earn rewards. That's what's live right now — with a lot more to come."

**Notes:**
- Don't call them "minigames" — just "games"
- Added "with a lot more to come" to signal the roadmap without overselling

### Block 3: "Who is it for?"

**Old:** "Kekspace is designed for users, projects, developers, communities, and companies who would like a Web3 native space that is both fun and functional."

**New (APPROVED):** "Gamers, communities, creators, and anyone who thinks virtual worlds should be fun again. If you're reading this, you're already the right audience."

**Notes:**
- Players/Developers sections from old slide are cut — too salesy for investor bible
- Ownership pitch intentionally held back for Economy section
- **Art direction:** Replace the kekspace_banner.png with a character lineup — a row of colorful, diverse NPCs standing side by side like a "meet the residents" strip. Pick the most vibrant and varied characters to show off the art style and personality of the world. Each character should represent a different zone/vibe. Render at consistent height on a shared baseline.
